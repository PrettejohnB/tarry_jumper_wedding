Our Story

Our love story began in February of 2018, when JP moved down to Cape Town and moved in with a mutual friend. Tarryn attended a braai at their house and after a couple glasses of wine and a bunch of beers later, they were the last two left at the party helping our friend disinfect a severely vomit-shot bathroom.
The next move was the gamechanger, and let’s just say JP had Tarryn at “Kittens”. All he had to do was mention that he was housesitting some kittens, and Tarryn was sold. The night was extended into a kitten visit, some coffee and more chatting.
The rest is history… 2 and a half years of love and laughter, a new house, 3 Kitties and an amazing Ireland and Amsterdam travel experience (and an amazingly romantic proposal in Vondel Park) later and we are ready for our next adventure to begin!
